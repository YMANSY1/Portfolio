Class Pulse

An educational ecosystem leveraging ChatGPT to personalise and simplify teaching experience for both students and teachers

About the Project

The biggest project I have ever worked on and my first major freelancing gig! Class Pulse is an app that works to leverage ChatGPT to generate personalised questions and help teachers correct assignments and quizzes by providing feedback. The project consists of 2 apps, one for students and parents and another for teachers. The teacher app allows assigning homeworks with the use of AI and correcting assignments, also giving the option to assign personalised revision depending on each students' individual weaknesses. The student and parents app allows tracking of exams and assignments, chatting between students, and solving assignments by teachers with grades being recorded on the app.

Teacher App

The teachers may assign homework or quizzes of any of the types displayed in this screen.

Assigning Questions

The teacher may manually assign the questions or assign them using AI. There are several variations of this screen based off of the type of assignment the teacher wants to assign. When the AI is done assigning the questions, the teacher has the option of reviewing, editing, and picking the questions they need. Foregoing the use of AI and manually assigning questions is also on the table, providing a degree of flexibility to the teachers and more control over their work.

AI Question Assignment Screen

The ability to add questions using ChatGPT is here, only using a prompt may the teacher add the questions they need of any type. Questions may be essay, MCQ, fill-in-the-blank, short answer, etc. The teacher may also assign chat and voice assignments, allowing students to talk to chatgpt and get feedback based of their conversational skills.

Student App

The student app allows social interaction between students, parents, and teachers. It mainly also allows students and parents to track performance and interact with assignments by the teachers. Shown here is a dashboard allowing students to see the assignment they should do, their deadline, their type, and their title. The student may press the assignment and solve it and submit it for correction.

Tasks Page

This page allows student to see all of their homeworks and choose which one to solve. Their submissions will be autocorrected by ChatGPT and sent back to the teachers for evaluation and finalization. The app uses firebase for the backend.

Final thoughts

This project allowed me to work with a dynamic and passionate team seeking to innovate in the rising field of AI and leverages ChatGPT in a very creative and useful way to streamline the jobs of both teacher and students. I learned Firebase as a backend service expanding my abilities alongside my previous experience with Supabase; in addition, I also learned how to interact with Figma designs and convert Figma designs into responsive and attractive Flutter UI. Finally, I learned MVVM architecture and how to make robust and maintainable code for use by my team. Working on this project has been a transformative experience for my career and increased my confidence and abilities exponentially!
