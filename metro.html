Cairo Metro Navigation App - IN PROGRESS

A lightweight Android and Flutter app designed to simplify your journey through the Cairo Metro

About the Project

Ever had trouble navigating through the Cairo Metro? Tired of having to constantly check the map to find out where to go? Well our navigation app has you covered with a simple and interactive UI that can provide you the with the quickest or most comfortable route you desire. Alongside other quality of life features like automatically detecting the closest station to you or allowing you to write in a location of your choosing and it will find the nearest station to you. This set of unique features makes this app a powerful yet simple tool that any person using the Metro needs to have installed.

Home Screen

When you first open the app you are presented with the home screen that includes a simple guide on how to use the app and resources to contact the Cairo metro including their hotline and email.

Planner Page

Next is the planner screen where all the fun happens! In this screen you can find the entry station and exit station fields. The entry station field is automatically autofilled with the nearest station to your location, the exit station field must either be filled by the user or it will be autofilled depending on the value written in the destination field at the very bottom. The destination field gives the user the option to write the location they wish to go to and the exit station field will be autofilled with nearest station to that location. As seen in the image, the user wrote 'manial' so the exit field contains the station 'El Malek El Saleh' as that is the closest station to the destination written.

Other features

The app includes more features such as the ability to view the map of the Cairo Metro through pressing the button in the app bar on the left, and the settings menu provides multiple options for customisation and tailoring the app to the individual needs of the user. The app is available in both Arabic and English and includes the option to opt for dark and light modes (not seen here as this feature is still being added).
